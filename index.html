Background

This visualization, which was produced for Education Above All (EAA), features an overview of children who are out of school in the following countries: the Democratic Republic of the Congo, India, Nigeria, Sudan, Syria, Yemen, Chad, Ethiopia, Iran, Kenya, Malaysia, Pakistan, Rwanda, South Sudan and Uganda. This visualization also shows which organisation are doing what and where to empower children to education. EAA is a global foundation protecting the right of children and youth to quality education. This visualization aggregates data sets from UNICEF, U.S. Fund for UNICEF, International Rescue Committee, Caritas, Oando Foundation, Educate Girls, UNHCR and HPPI. This visualization captures data from the time period: 1st of January 2012 through the 31st of December 2016.
